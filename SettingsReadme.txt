TerrainNoiseType Options = Perlin,OpenSimplex2, TerrainAmplitude = TerrainHeight scaled from 0-1, All counts are scaled to a 256x256 map size, except mines which can have a minimum no matter how small.  All the rest should be self-explanatory.  TerrainSlopeLevel is a confusing value, but basically, lower values equal less slope but also lower the overall terrain height (you may need to adjust your river for this.
